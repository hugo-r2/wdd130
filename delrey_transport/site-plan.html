Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide our customers with all the information about our long-distance transportation company (by land, around our whole country: Guatemala), the types of vehicles that they can request, and the locations we can take them to. They will also find all the details they need to contact us and request our services.

Audience

Our target audience is any large group of people that need to travel a long distance either for vacation, a school trip, or a business trip. This can include families, religious groups, small school groups etc. Each vehicle can fit around 12-15 people.

Content

Home page

Take a moment to think about our beautiful country, all those impressive landscapes, beautiful cities and villages. Do you want to know any of them? Or you do and you want to re-visit an amazing place you fell in love with? No worries! We got you covered.

We are here to make those desires come true. Our vehicles and drivers are ready to make sure you get to those wonderful locations without having to worry about driving many hours to get there. You can enjoy the view while we take you to your destination!

This is the perfect option for when you are planning to go long distances with large groups of people. You can use up to 3 of our vehicles at a time, which means up to 42 people can go with you on the same trip!*

* (This may vary depending on specific needs).

Locations

Here you will find a list of the most common places we travel to, keep in mind that these are just the popular ones, so do not hesitate to ask if your destination is not listed here.

Port of San Jose

This is one of the most common requests because the beach is one of the most comfortable places to enjoy vacation. The tropical weather can be enjoyed in any season of the year, the abundant seafood, and the convenient closeness to Guatemala City, make this location the perfect choice for a one-day trip.

Antigua Guatemala City

If you want to know more about our history, this city is one of the best choices. You will never get tired of the vintage vibes you will be getting the whole time. This city was the original “Guatemala city” the first location of our capital city. The architecture has remained the same ever since it was founded. It is like traveling through time!

Panajachel (Atitlán Lake)

Many say that Atitlan Lake is the most beautiful lake in the world, and they may not be mistaken. Come find out yourself by visiting this magic village called Panajachel, located just at the lakeshore. Enjoy the beautiful landscape of this magic lake, and forget the stress of the everyday tasks for a moment.

Quiriguá Ruins

Find yourself trapped in the mysteries of the Mayan culture. These are one of the most famous Mayan ruins in the whole country, due to the nature of its architecture. It has very tall stand-alone monuments called “Estelas”. One of them (Estela “A”) is considered the tallest stand-alone monument in the whole American continent.

Frequently asked Questions

How many people can travel at the same time?

You can have a group as big as 42 people. This is because you can dispose of up to 3 vans at the same time, which can take an average of 14 people each. This can vary depending on specific needs. Do not hesitate to contact us for more specific questions.

What if our group is too small?

Don’t worry! There are no small groups. Even though we don’t have any smaller vehicles, you can contact us for more information about the services we offer regardless of the number of people that will travel.

What type of vehicles are used?

At the moment we have 2 different types of vehicles. We can either use a Hyundai H100, or a Mitsubishi L300. You can see pictures of each at the bottom of the home page.

What if the destination I want to go to is not listed?

Don’t worry! You can go to the Contact Us section and ask us directly if we can take you where you want to go.

Can I drive myself or hire an external driver?

All of our services include a driver. For security purposes only our designated drivers are entitled to use the company’s vehicles.
